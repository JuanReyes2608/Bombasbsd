WHO ARE WE?

We are an oilfield services company with deep knowledge of fluids in the oil industry value chain, with more than 15 years of experience in SEEPEX technology, developing optimal solutions and working with our main client PEMEX.

OUR OBJECTIVE

BSD's main objective is to be part of the Seepex family, strengthening its growth strategy in Mexico by becoming the first authorized Seepex Service Center, focused on the Oil & Gas market segment, optimizing energy use for extraction through the pressure reduction system method using progressive cavity pumps, reducing aging in mature fields, and developing excellent, reliable, and cost-effective solutions using Seepex technology, a world leader in progressive cavity pumps.

Specialists in Hydraulic, Rotodynamic, and Positive Displacement Machines.
